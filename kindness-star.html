How to become a Kindness Star

A simple, transparent process — here's exactly what happens.

1

Apply

Fill in the short application form — about two minutes. It's by invitation, so you're already on our radar.

2

Create your kindness video

Film something real — a kind act, what kindness means to you, or weave it into your usual style. One minute or longer.

3

Post it — and share it everywhere

Post on Gan Jing World with #BeKindBeCool, then share it to your own channels (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok) so your audience catches the kindness too.

4

Get celebrated

Selected Kindness Stars are featured across Gan Jing World, showcased on the Times Square billboard, and entered for the $10,000 prize. Winner announced Spring 2027.
